ABOUT PRACTIPRO

• PractiPro is an innovative platform that streamlines the management of student internships by digitizing paperwork and facilitating collaboration between students, educators and administrators. It offers digital evaluation and reporting tools to provide insights into program and student performance, helping students on their path to professional success.

• OUR OBJECTIVES:

To provide clear directions and centralization of practicum requirements and submissions, along with the ability to upload a variety of file formats easily.
Allow smooth communication between teachers and students. Facilitate interaction, respond to inquiries, and offer an opportunity for users to provide constructive feedback.
Give students the ability to monitor their progress on practicum submissions. Encourage students to fulfill their Practicum assignments and expectations by increasing accessibility and obligation.
To reduce the amount of paperwork and effort required for the traditional practicum process, allowing instructors and administrators to digitally complete evaluation forms within the platform based on information gathered through the platform. Handle reports on program performance, practicum layouts, and student documentation to facilitate the digitalized evaluation of students during their practicum process.
