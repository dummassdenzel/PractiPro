ABOUT PRACTIPRO

an innovative platform that streamlines the management of student internships by digitizing paperwork and facilitating collaboration between students, educators, and industry partners. It offers digital evaluation and reporting tools to provide insights into program and student performance, helping students on their path to professional success.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Provide clear directions and deadlines, along with the ability to upload a variety of file formats easily.
Allow smooth communication between teachers and students. Facilitate interaction, respond to inquiries, and offer a forum for constructive feedback
Give students the ability to monitor their progress on practicum submissions. Encourage students to fulfill deadlines and expectations by increasing accessibility and obligation
To reduce the amount of paperwork and save time, allow instructors and administrators to digitally complete evaluation forms within the platform. Based on information gathered through the platform, provide reports on program performance, practicum layouts, and student performance.
To reduce the amount of time and paperwork required for traditional practicum processes., allowing instructors and administrators to digitally complete evaluation forms within the platform. providing reports on program performance, practicum layouts, and student performance based on information gathered through the platform.
